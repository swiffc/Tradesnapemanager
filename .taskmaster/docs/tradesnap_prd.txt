## üéØ Executive Summary
TradeSnapManager is a comprehensive trading screenshot management system designed for systematic trade analysis and performance tracking. The application enables traders to organize, analyze, and learn from their trading screenshots using a structured **Bias ‚Üí Setup ‚Üí Pattern ‚Üí Entry** methodology.

**Vision Statement**: To provide traders with the most comprehensive and systematic trading screenshot management platform that transforms scattered trade images into actionable insights and improved performance.

**Mission**: Empower traders to achieve consistent profitability through systematic documentation, analysis, and continuous improvement of their trading strategies.

## üéØ Problem Statement
**Current Pain Points**:
1. **Disorganized Trade Screenshots**: Traders accumulate hundreds of screenshots without systematic organization
2. **Lack of Performance Analysis**: No centralized way to analyze win rates, risk-reward ratios across strategies

## üöÄ Core Features

### 1. Screenshot Management System (P0 - Critical)
- Upload multiple trading screenshots (drag-and-drop, file browser)
- Support common image formats (PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP)
- Automatic image optimization and compression
- Cloud storage integration (Supabase Storage)
- Image preview with zoom capabilities
- Bulk upload functionality

### 2. Systematic Trade Categorization (P0 - Critical)
**Hierarchical Structure**: Bias ‚Üí Setup ‚Üí Pattern ‚Üí Entry

**Bias Categories**:
- **Levels Folder**: M, A1, A2, W, V1, V2
  - M: M pattern on H1 timeframe and higher before a down move
  - A1: First stophunt high level in the down move
  - A2: Second stophunt high level in the down move  
  - W: Reversal pattern before the big up move
  - V1: First stophunt low level (Level 1)
  - V2: Second stophunt low level (Level 2)
- **Other Categories**: ABS, 3XADR, L1_13_50, L2_50_200
**Setup Categories**: Box Setups, Anchors, Asian Range, Harmonics P1, Reset Safety, Resets
**Pattern Categories**: 1H 50 50 Bounce, 2nd leg half bat, 3 Drives - 3 Day setups, 3 Hits Trade, Half Bats, Head and Shoulders Patterns, ID 50, London Patterns, M-Formation, type1, type2, type3, type4, W_Formation
**Entry Types**: a/b/c variants, Railroad Tracks, Cord of Woods, Evening Star, Morning Star, Shift Candle

### 3. Performance Analytics Dashboard (P0 - Critical)
- Win/Loss ratios by strategy
- Risk-Reward analysis (R-multiples)
- Session performance comparison (Asian, London, NY)
- Consecutive wins/losses tracking
- Drawdown analysis
- Currency pair performance
- Interactive charts using Recharts

### 4. Session-Based Analysis (P1 - High)
**Sessions**: Asian (00:00-08:00 GMT), London (08:00-16:00 GMT), NY (13:00-22:00 GMT), Gap Times, Brinks
- Session-specific win rates
- Best performing setups per session
- Risk-reward comparison across sessions

### 5. Advanced Filtering & Search (P1 - High)
- Date range, currency pairs, trade outcomes
- Risk-reward ranges, strategy combinations
- Full-text search in notes
- Saved filter presets
